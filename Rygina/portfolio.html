I Carried Their Love as My Fuel: My Journey of Resilience and Faith

Shy to Limitless: Eagerly Exploring Life to the Fullest.

A Testimony of Unyielding Hope

I am Rygina Mae Bolante, and I carry the vibrant spirit of Sapang Binangonan, Rizal Philippines, in my heart. My life's story began not with ease, but with a profound lesson: that true strength is discovered in the climb.

Though I grew up without my parents, that absence never created a void. Instead, it became the space where grace poured in. My anchor was my grandmother's unconditional love—a fierce, protective devotion that was my first and greatest gift.

The path was illuminated by the kindness of others and fortified by the unshakeable grace of God. These were not chance encounters, but powerful reminders that I was never alone.

I stand here today as a living testimony: Hardship is not a destiny, but a forge. It is where we temper our spirit and discover the incredible capacity we have to keep going. I am proof that with love and faith as your foundation, you can turn any challenging beginning into a compelling story of triumph and purpose.
